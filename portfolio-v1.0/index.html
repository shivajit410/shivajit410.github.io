About Me

I am a simple, creative, enthusiastic and fun-loving person. I believe doing things in the best possible way! I keep a problem solving attitude towards my work. Whenever I face problem, all I do is breakdown it into pieces so that it does not seem intimidating.

I am born and bought up from Maharashtra, India. I am a graduate from Electronics department. After graduation I started working as Programmer Analyst and recently I've started taking up freelancing jobs. When not coding, you'll find me binge-watching, hanging out with friends or cooking!
